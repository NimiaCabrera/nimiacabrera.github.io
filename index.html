About the Designer

As it is in any design process, I feel that my work is a working progress. I am a fast leaner who is fueled by Arnold Palmer’s and would love the opportunity to expand my skills in a working environment.

Great as a team member or working independently. I am determined to become a substantial part of a team of creatives and take what I learn to grow as a designer.
